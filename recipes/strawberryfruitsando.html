Strawberry Fruit Sando

Japanese Fruit Sandwich

This Japanese fruit sandwich (strawberry fruit sando) is a refreshing dessert that can be brought to picnics, parties, and more.

The ingredients are very easy to obtain, and it doesn't take much effort to make.

Ingredients

  • 4 slices bread
  • 1 cup heavy whipping cream
  • 3/2 tbsp sugar
  • 14 strawberries

Directions

  1. Wash and prep strawberries. Cut stems and dry thoroughly.
  2. Whip heavy cream and sugar until it forms stiff peaks. It should be soft, fluffy and hold a peak.
  3. Spread the whipped cream on a slice of break (about 1/2 in thick). Arrange the strawberries so that it reveals the design when cut into.
  4. Spread more whipped cream on top of the strawberries to fill in gaps. Top with another slice of bread.
  5. (Optional) Wrap sandwich in plastic wrap and let sit in the fridge (recommended 20 min).
  6. Unwrap sandwich and remove the crust. Cut in desired way and serve.
