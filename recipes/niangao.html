Niangao

Grandma's Niangao

This is the best Niangao you will ever eat. And taste. You can slice it up, pan fry it, or boil it!

This recipe was passed down from my grandma. It is a very traditional dessert that many Asians eat during the Lunar New Year. Enjoy!

Ingredients

  • 1 bag glutinous rice flour (green bag)
  • 1 bag rice flour (red bag)
  • 3.5 sticks black sugar
  • Up to 2.5 cups water

Directions

  1. Dissolve black sugar in 1 cup water
  2. Let black sugar cool
  3. Mix flours together
  4. Add the dissolved sugar to the flours
  5. Slowly add up to 1.5 cups water and mix until it reaches a watery yet smooth consistency
  6. Steam the mixture for 35 mins
  7. Let it cool
