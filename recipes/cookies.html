Chocolate Chip Cookies

So, why this recipe?

Craving cookies? No, not the cookies browsers ask you to accept. Obviously. Well, if you aren't looking for those cookies, you've come to the right spot.

This cookie recipe will rejuvenate you. The crunchy exterior and soft, chocolatey, chewy interior will leave you begging for more (once you finished them of course).

Ingredients

  • 1 cup salted butter (softened)
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 1 cup brown sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1/2 tsp baking powder
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 2 cups chocolate chips
  1. Preheat oven to 375 F. Line a baking pan w/ parchment paper. Set aside.
  2. In a separate bowl, mix four, baking soda, salt, and baking soda. Set aside.
  3. Cream together butter and sugars until combined.
  4. Beat/whisk in eggs and vanilla until fluffy.
  5. Mix in dry ingredients until combined.
  6. Add chocolate chips. Mix well.
  7. Roll 2-3 tbs of dough at a time into balls and evenly space them on the baking pan.
  8. Bake for 8-10 mins. Take them out when they are just barely starting to turn brown.
  9. Let them sit on the baking pan for 2 mins before removing to a cooling rack.
